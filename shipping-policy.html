Shipping Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

We are committed to delivering your orders swiftly, safely, and reliably across India. Our logistics network covers 20,000+ pin codes through trusted courier partners. Please review our shipping policy to understand processing times, rates, and delivery timelines.

Terms & Conditions Privacy Policy Refund & Returns Cancellation Policy Shipping Policy
01

Order Processing Times

All orders are processed within 1 to 2 business days after you receive your order confirmation email (excluding weekends and public holidays). You will receive a shipping confirmation with tracking details once your order has been dispatched.

📋 Order Placed Confirmation email sent immediately
📦 Processing 1–2 business days
🚚 Dispatched Tracking link sent via SMS & email
02

Domestic Shipping Rates & Delivery Estimates

Shipping charges are calculated based on your order value and delivery location. The following rates apply across India:

Shipping Option Estimated Delivery Time Charges
Standard Delivery (Orders ≥ ₹500) 3 to 7 business days FREE
Standard Delivery (Orders < ₹500) 3 to 7 business days ₹50 flat rate
Express Delivery (Metro cities only) 1 to 2 business days ₹150
Remote / Rural Locations 5 to 10 business days ₹80 flat rate

* Metro cities include Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Chennai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Pune, and Ahmedabad.

03

Our Courier Partners

We ship via trusted, pan-India courier partners to ensure reliable and timely delivery:

🚛 Delhivery 🚛 BlueDart 🚛 Ecom Express 🚛 DTDC 🚛 XpressBees 🚛 India Post

The courier partner assigned to your order may vary based on your delivery location and the nature of the product.

04

Cash on Delivery (COD)

We offer Cash on Delivery (COD) to 20,000+ pin codes across India. Please note the following regarding COD orders:

  • An additional handling fee of ₹40 is applied to all COD orders.
  • COD availability and limits may vary by product category and order value.
  • Please keep the exact amount ready at the time of delivery.
  • COD is not available for orders above ₹10,000 or for certain product categories.
05

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive an email and SMS notification with your tracking number and a direct link to the courier's tracking page.

Please allow 24–48 hours after dispatch for the tracking information to become active on the courier's website.

ⓘ If you haven't received your order within 7 days of your shipping confirmation, please contact us at delivery@anon.in with your name and Order ID.

06

P.O. Boxes & Restricted Addresses

Our courier partners typically require a signature upon delivery and cannot ship to P.O. Boxes. Please ensure you provide a complete residential or commercial address, including flat/house number, street name, landmark, city, state, and pin code.

07

Delays & Exceptions

While we strive to meet all delivery timelines, unforeseen circumstances may occasionally cause delays. These include:

  • National holidays or regional public holidays.
  • Severe weather conditions, natural disasters, or force majeure events.
  • Local strikes, curfews, or disruptions at pin code level.
  • High-volume periods (e.g., festivals, sale events).

We will proactively communicate any significant delays to your registered email or phone number.
