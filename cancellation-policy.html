Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At Anon eCommerce, we aim to dispatch your orders as quickly as possible. Our cancellation policy is designed to be transparent and fair. Please read it carefully to understand your options before and after your order is placed.

Terms & Conditions Privacy Policy Refund & Returns Cancellation Policy Shipping Policy
01

Cancellation by Customer

You may cancel your order under the following conditions:

✔ Cancellable — Before Dispatch

Orders can be cancelled entirely, free of charge, provided they have not yet been dispatched from our warehouse.

✘ Not Cancellable — After Dispatch

Once dispatched, cancellation is not possible. Please wait for delivery and initiate a return per our Returns Policy.

02

How to Cancel an Order

There are two ways to cancel your order (before dispatch):

  • Via Account Dashboard: Log in to your account, go to "My Orders", select the order you wish to cancel, and click "Cancel Order".
  • Via Customer Support: Email us at support@anon.in or call +91 98765 43210 with your Order ID. Support is available Monday – Saturday, 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM IST.
03

Cancellation by Anon eCommerce

We reserve the right to cancel any order under the following circumstances:

  • Products being out of stock or discontinued at the time of processing.
  • Pricing or typographical errors on the website.
  • Suspected fraudulent activities or bulk purchases that violate our Terms and Conditions.
  • Non-serviceable delivery locations or incorrect address provided.
  • Failure of payment authorisation or suspected payment fraud.

In all such cases, we will inform you of the cancellation via email and provide a full refund if payment has already been captured.

04

Refunds for Cancelled Orders

If your order is successfully cancelled before dispatch, we will initiate a full refund to your original method of payment. You will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been processed.

The refund amount should reflect in your bank account, credit card, or digital wallet within 5–7 business days, depending on your bank's processing timelines.

ⓘ For UPI payments, refunds are typically processed within 24–48 hours. For credit/debit cards, it may take up to 7 working days. You will receive an email notification as soon as the refund is initiated.

05

Custom or Personalised Orders

Orders for personalised, custom-made, embroidered, or print-on-demand items cannot be cancelled once the production process has begun. Please double-check your customisation details (size, name, design) before placing such orders.

06

Contact Us

For any questions or concerns about your cancellation, please reach out to us:

Email: support@anon.in

Phone: +91 98765 43210
