Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At Anon eCommerce, we are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and make purchases from us. Please read this policy carefully.

Terms & Conditions Privacy Policy Refund & Returns Cancellation Policy Shipping Policy
01

Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when you register on the website, make a purchase, or contact us. This includes:

  • Personal Identifiers: Name, email address, phone number, and billing/shipping address.
  • Payment Information: Card details or UPI IDs processed securely via our payment gateway partners (Razorpay, PayU, etc.). We do not store complete payment credentials on our servers.
  • Account Data: Username, password, purchase history, and wishlist information.
  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, pages visited, and time spent on the site.
02

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect or receive to:

  • Facilitate account creation and the login process.
  • Process and fulfil your orders, payments, returns, and exchanges.
  • Send you order confirmation, shipping updates, and delivery notifications.
  • Send marketing and promotional communications (only with your consent).
  • Respond to your enquiries and provide customer support.
  • Improve our website, products, and services based on your feedback.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraudulent activities.
03

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We only share your personal information in the following circumstances:

  • Delivery Partners: We share your name, address, and phone number with our courier partners (e.g., Delhivery, BlueDart, Ecom Express) solely to facilitate order delivery.
  • Payment Processors: We share transaction data with our payment gateway partners to securely process payments. These partners operate under their own privacy policies.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

04

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies (such as web beacons and pixels) to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and analyse website traffic. Cookies help us:

  • Keep you logged into your account.
  • Remember your shopping cart contents.
  • Understand which pages are most popular.
  • Deliver relevant advertisements.

You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, some features of the Site may not function properly without cookies.

05

Data Security

We have implemented appropriate technical and organisational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. We use SSL encryption for all data transmissions and store sensitive information in encrypted form. However, no electronic transmission over the internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

06

Your Privacy Rights

In accordance with the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India), you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal information held by us.
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact our Grievance Officer at privacy@anon.in.

07

Contact Us

If you have questions, comments, or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact our Data Protection / Grievance Officer:

Email: privacy@anon.in
