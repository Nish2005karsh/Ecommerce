Refund & Returns Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

We want you to be completely satisfied with every purchase at Anon eCommerce. If you are not entirely happy with your item for any reason, we offer a straightforward 15-day return window from the date of final delivery. Please read the policy below carefully before initiating a return.

Terms & Conditions Privacy Policy Refund & Returns Cancellation Policy Shipping Policy
01

Return Window

You may return eligible items within 15 days from the date of final delivery. Returns requested after this window will not be accepted.

📦 Step 1

Initiate return via "My Orders" in your account within 15 days of delivery.

🚚 Step 2

Our courier partner picks up the package from your address within 2–4 business days.

✅ Step 3

Refund is processed within 5–7 business days after quality inspection.

02

Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, the following conditions must be met:

  • The item must be unused, unwashed, and unaltered — in the exact condition you received it.
  • The item must be in its original packaging with all tags, labels, and protective seals intact.
  • You must provide the original invoice or electronic proof of purchase.
  • The return must be initiated within the 15-day window.

⚠ Non-returnable items: Perishable goods, intimate/sanitary wear (innerwear, swimwear), hazardous materials, gift cards, and personalised or customised products are not eligible for return.

03

Quality Check & Refund Processing

Once we receive your returned item, our quality assurance team will inspect it within 2 business days. You will be notified of the inspection outcome via email and SMS.

  • If the return is approved, your refund will be processed to the original payment method — credit/debit card, UPI, net banking, or wallet.
  • Refunds are typically credited within 5–7 business days, depending on your bank's processing timelines.
  • For Cash on Delivery (COD) orders, refunds will be transferred via NEFT/IMPS to your designated bank account, or as store credit at your preference.
04

Defective or Damaged Products

If you receive a product that is damaged, defective, or significantly different from what was described, please notify us within 48 hours of delivery with photographic or video evidence of the issue.

In such cases, we will arrange a free-of-cost replacement or a full refund — whichever you prefer — with no return shipping charge.

05

Return Shipping

We provide free return pick-up for all eligible returns. Our delivery partner will collect the package from your registered delivery address within 2–4 business days of your return request being approved.

In cases of multiple recurring returns without valid reasons, we reserve the right to apply an adjustment fee for return logistics or restrict future return privileges.

06
